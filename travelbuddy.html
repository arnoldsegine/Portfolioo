"Travel that understands you."

The Problem

Generic results fail everyone.

Through independent user research, I identified a clear market gap. Platforms like Google Maps and Yelp return generic results with no understanding of identity specific travel needs including culture, faith, language, grooming, or diaspora services.

A Black traveler in Copenhagen should not have to scroll through 40 irrelevant barbers. A Muslim traveler in Florence deserves to find a hijabi friendly salon without guesswork.

🔍African grocery store in LisbonNo results
The Solution

AI that understands context.

TravelBuddy AI uses LLM powered natural language search to interpret traveler identity context and return relevant, culturally tagged recommendations.

The AI parses location, service type, identity needs, cultural context, and language preferences then surfaces the right businesses with an explanation for each recommendation.

MVP Features
Natural language search

User Flow

Four steps to feeling seen.

01

Type your need

User types a travel need in plain natural language with no filters or dropdowns needed.

02

AI interprets

LLM parses location, identity, cultural context, faith, and language from the query.

03

System searches

Searches identity tagged business dataset to find the most relevant matches.

04

Personalized results

User receives ranked recommendations with AI explanations for each result.
