The Problem

What needed solving

Sales teams had no real time visibility into which prospects to prioritize. Manual qualification processes wasted cycles and there was no unified view of lead engagement data.

Users

Who it was built for

Sales representatives, sales managers, and revenue operations teams who needed data driven insights to prioritize outreach and improve conversion.

The Solution

How we solved it

Designed a lead intelligence dashboard in Tableau surfacing real time insights from multiple data sources. Enabled teams to prioritize actions based on live engagement data and performance trends.

My Role

What I owned

Product Manager responsible for requirements definition, stakeholder research with sales teams, feature prioritization, and coordinating with the data engineering team.
